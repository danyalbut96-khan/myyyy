Data Collection & Ingestion

We collect diagnostic data, imaging files, and clinical notes solely for the purpose of generating AI-driven insights. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 standards and in transit via TLS 1.3.

  • Patient Identifiable Information (PII) is automatically de-identified before entering the AI core.
  • Metadata including timestamps and diagnostic equipment IDs are logged for auditing purposes.
  • User interaction data is tracked to improve diagnostic accuracy.

AI Processing Ethics

MediFinder AI employs a "Zero-Trust Inference" model. Your clinical data is never used to train global models shared with other healthcare institutions without explicit, granular consent.

Local Inference

All initial screenings are performed on localized, sandboxed environments.

Audit Trail

Every AI suggestion is timestamped and linked to its original diagnostic source.

HIPAA Compliance

MediFinder AI is fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Our systems undergo quarterly third-party audits to ensure the integrity of protected health information (PHI).

"Clinical integrity is maintained through rigorous administrative, physical, and technical safeguards." - Chief Compliance Officer

Data Retention

Records are retained in accordance with medical statutory requirements (typically 7 years for adults). Users may request data purging at any time, subject to legal overrides regarding clinical record keeping.
