CLINICAL SECURITY PROTOCOL

MediFinder AI utilizes localized encryption protocols. We do not store identifiable patient data on central servers. All medical search queries are anonymized before processing through our neural networks.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect data necessary to provide and improve our medical information retrieval services. This includes search terms, dosage queries, and interaction patterns within the interface. We prioritize non-personally identifiable information (Non-PII) to maintain your digital anonymity.

Usage Data

Aggregated statistics on medicine search frequency and category selection used for system optimization.

Technical Logs

IP addresses (masked), browser types, and timestamp data used solely for threat prevention and security monitoring.

API Key and Authentication

MediFinder AI interfaces with various pharmaceutical databases and LLM providers. For developers and institutional users:

  • lock_open API keys are stored using hardware-level encryption (AES-256).
  • cloud_off Personal API keys are never cached in browser local storage; they exist only within secure session states.
  • history Access logs for API interactions are automatically purged every 24 hours.

Medical Accuracy and Disclaimer

While we protect your data, the information provided by MediFinder AI is for informational purposes only. Our privacy protocols ensure that your inquiries about specific conditions or medications remain confidential.

User Rights & Erasure

Under modern data protection regulations, you maintain full control over your digital footprint within our system. You have the right to request a full export of your interaction history or the permanent erasure of any linked data points.
